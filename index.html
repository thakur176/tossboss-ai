🧠 How to Use TossBoss AI

Enter two lists: one in each column. TossBoss AI will randomly match the items across the columns to help you decide or play in a smarter way — whether you're planning meals, deciding study subjects, picking ranks, or just having fun!

📌 Use Cases

  1. 🎮 Decide player ranks for games
  2. 👗 Help girls choose outfits
  3. 📅 Decide what to play on which days
  4. 🍲 Plan meals for the week
  5. 📚 Randomly assign study subjects per day
  6. 🎉 Add fun to decision-making during parties
